About Me

Hi! I am Joy Cortes
I am a dedicated individual on a journey to becoming a licensed professional teacher. Passionate about the transformative power of education, I am currently immersed in the process of acquiring the skills and knowledge necessary to make a positive impact in the classroom. My approach to teaching combines a solid foundation of theoretical understanding with practical experiences, allowing me to create dynamic lesson plans and employ innovative teaching methods. Beyond the academic realm, I am committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment that encourages student growth and development. Eager to contribute to the field of education, I am actively engaged in ongoing professional development to stay current with the latest trends and techniques. As I pursue my license in professional teaching, my goal is to inspire and empower students, instilling in them a lifelong love for learning. I am excited about the opportunity to make a meaningful difference in the lives of my students and contribute to the advancement of education.
